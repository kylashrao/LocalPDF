About LocalPDF

Most "free" PDF tools online work the same way: you upload your file to their server, their server processes it, and you download the result. That means a copy of whatever you uploaded — a contract, a tax form, a medical record, a passport scan — passes through someone else's infrastructure before you get it back.

LocalPDF was built to remove that step entirely. Every tool here runs inside your browser using your device's own processing power. Your file is read, modified, and saved without ever being sent anywhere.

🔒

Nothing uploaded

Your file is processed using your browser's own memory and CPU. It never touches a server.

⚡

Works offline

Once a tool's page has loaded, you can disconnect from the internet and it keeps working.

🆓

Free, no limits

No file count caps, no size throttling, no account walls — just open a tool and use it.

How this is technically possible

Modern browsers are capable of running real document-processing code, not just displaying pages. LocalPDF loads an open-source PDF engine directly into the page you're viewing, and all merging, splitting, and compressing happens using that engine running locally on your device. The result is downloaded straight from your browser's memory — at no point does your file leave your machine.

Who this is for

LocalPDF is used by anyone who wants to combine, extract, or shrink PDF files without handing them to a third-party server — freelancers preparing client documents, students assembling assignments, and anyone working with anything they'd rather keep private.
